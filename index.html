About

I'm an integrated vascular surgery resident at Henry Ford Health Providence Hospital and Michigan State University College of Human Medicine. I completed my medical degree at McGovern Medical School at UTHealth in Houston and my undergraduate degree in biology at the University of Houston.

My clinical and academic interests include minimally invasive and endovascular surgery, aortic disease, surgical ergonomics, and the influence of social determinants of health on vascular surgical outcomes.

Outside of medicine, I've spent over a decade in entrepreneurship as co-founder of TUR Party Supplies, a direct-to-consumer e-commerce company, with hands-on experience in e-commerce, digital marketing, and product and graphic design.
